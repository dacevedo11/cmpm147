Experiment 1 - Getting Started

Dad Outfit Generator

Translated from dad-outfits on glitch.com

Description

The goal of this experimnent was to create a unique generator tool and implementing that tool into my portfolio website.

Technical

I used a grammar based text generator to generate a variety of outcomes.

Reflection

This experiment wasn't particularly difficult, but it felt very time consuming. Maybe it's just because we're in the beginning of the class, but I had to spend a lot of time just making everything look the way I wanted to, and I didn't do anything particularly special. Although, I felt this exercise was a good introduction to procedural generation and what kinds of things could be done using it.
