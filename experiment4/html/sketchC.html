Description

The chess board extends infinitely, only limited by the amount of moves the pawn can make.Instead of drawing the pieces, I'm using an image of a pawn piece and the npc pieces are all the opposite color. The placement of the opposing pieces is determined randomly using the user's key. In theory, the player is able to move their pawn and take other opposing pawns.
